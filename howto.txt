Create a manim scene by:

~ gomanim


You can run Manim by:

~ manim [script.py] [Class name]

or with

~ python -m manim [script.py] [Class name]


Video settings:

Low quality:
~ manim -pql [script.py] [Class name]

High quality:
~ manim -pqk [scripty.py] [Class name]


Create gif:

~ manim --format gif [scene.py] [Class name]
